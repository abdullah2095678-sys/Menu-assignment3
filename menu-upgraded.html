Our Story

The Spirit of the Izakaya

居酒屋の精神
炎
Since 2019

Where Ember Meets
Winter Night

Kuroyuki — meaning "black snow" — was born from one vision: to recreate the soul of a Tokyo back-alley izakaya in the heart of Toronto. Our robata grill burns with white-oak charcoal from Wakayama Prefecture, and every dish honours the Japanese principle of ma — the beauty of what is left unsaid.

🌿 Vegetarian Friendly🌊 Fresh Daily Fish🍶 75+ Sakes

Our chefs train under the omakase tradition. We source Wagyu from Miyazaki, salmon from Hokkaido, and tofu pressed fresh each morning in our kitchen.

Low light, warm cedar, quiet jazz, and the hiss of skewers over coals. This is not just a restaurant. It is an evening.
